Commands:

- /marry
  - The marry command line
- /marry player {Username}
  - Send a marry request or accept a marry request.
  - May require permission: marriage.marry
- /marry partner
  - See who you're married to.
- /marry tp
  - Teleports the player to their married partner.
  - May require permission: marriage.tp
- /marry msg {msg}
  - Send a private message to your married partner.
  - May require permission: marriage.msg
- /marry divorce
  - Divorce your partner, has a confirmation.
  - May require permission: marriage.divorce
- /marry list
  - Get a list of all married players that are currently on the server.
