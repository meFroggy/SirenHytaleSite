### Welcome to the Official [Orbis Origins X Mermaids](https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/orbis-origins-mermaids) Mod Wiki!

This mod adds a Mermaid species to [Orbis Origins] while using the [Mermaids] mod as the bases.

Requires the [Orbis Origins] and [Mermaids] mod!
